Moodwee – Mood Tracking App

   

UI/UX • WordPress • Custom Plugin

 
   
   

Overview

   

Moodwee is a mood tracking web app designed to help users monitor emotional wellness. I created the UI design in soft, calming tones and built a custom WordPress plugin to store moods in a database and visualize them with charts.

   

My Role

   
         
  • UI/UX Design in Figma
    •      
  • Front-end development with HTML, CSS, WordPress Elementor
    •      
  • Custom plugin development for mood tracking
    •    
    ---    

Mood Board & Visual Identity

   

The visual identity was designed to be **calm, supportive, and non-intimidating**. My mood board established the foundational elements for the brand, including the color palette, typography, and overall tone.

   
         
   
         
  • **Color Palette:** Soft purples, pinks, and earthy tones were chosen to convey serenity and emotional balance.
    •      
  • **Typography:** I selected the clean, modern, and highly legible **Poppins** font for its friendly and accessible feel.
    •      
  • **Keywords:** The emotional keywords like **FEEL**, **SELECT**, **LOG**, and **TRACK** guided the user flow and messaging on the website.
    •    
    ---    

Logo Creation: Mood + Wee

   

The logo design process focused on combining the concepts of **moods** and **wellness** in a simple, friendly icon. The name 'Moodwee' itself suggests a lighthearted, personal approach to tracking.

   
         
   

The final logo is a combination of a circular **face symbol** (representing the user's mood) and a **wavy element** (representing the 'Wee' or the natural up-and-down flow of emotions). The result is a simple, abstract icon that is instantly recognizable and conveys the core purpose of the app.

    ---    

Website Design & Development

   

I used the established visual identity to design the main web application interface in Figma. The design is **clean and minimal**, prioritizing a clear call-to-action: **"Track Your Mood, Transform Your Mind."**

   

The front-end was built on **WordPress** using **Elementor** for structure, ensuring responsiveness and easy content management. The core functionality—the mood tracking and visualization—was handled by a **custom-developed WordPress plugin** built with PHP and JavaScript, creating a seamless user experience that is fully integrated into the website.

    ---    

Process

   

I started with user research → wireframes → UI mockups → plugin development. The goal was to keep the interface simple and motivational.

   
     
             
     
             
   
   

Results

   

- Created a functional app with personalized wellness tips & reminders.
       - Integrated graphs & downloadable reports.
       - Received positive feedback on clean UI and usability.
